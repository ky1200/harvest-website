Our Story

A home away
from home.

At Harvest Confinement Centre (爱心陪月中心), we believe every new mother deserves rest, nourishment, and heartfelt support during her confinement month.

Our home-based centres blend time-honoured Chinese confinement traditions with modern nutritional care — from freshly prepared herbal meals to gentle baby care guidance, every detail is thoughtfully managed.

Whether you're a first-time mum or welcoming your second child, our experienced team provides a calm, hygienic, and supportive environment so you can truly focus on recovery and bonding.
